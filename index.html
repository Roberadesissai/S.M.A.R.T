BOOK

The Book that helps me my learning and gets motivated in all my classes and to get a better understanding of all courses is"The New Science Of Learning" by Terry Doyle and Todd Zakrajsek. This Book talks about how there are many ways to understand what you learn in class or anywhere, And it shows you the best strategy to use in any circumstance. One of the strategies I found most interesting that I start using is "Find a pattern in learning," so basically, the strategy is about how you connect what you learn with something and find what makes them the same or different if you can identify that you understand. This is one of many things that I have learned from this Book; there is a lot you should read this Book if you want to understand your learning differently and efficiently. There is another book that helped me as well, and this Book is called "College Student Skills". This Book tells you how to manage your time, stress, take notes, and so on. I recommend those books if you want to move forward in your success.

Strategies

In the book "The New Science Of Learning," there are many ideas or strategies to try. One of the strategies that I use is "Pattern and learning", "Mindsets toward learning," and "Paying attention." There helps me a lot; the Pattern and learning are about how to find ways in what you learn and try to connect with the thing you know. I you this idea in one of my classes trying to find the Patterns between my understanding and the natural world projects help prepare me for my future job. The second idea is Mindsets help me to be constant in my learning, which means if I set my mind that I can pass all my classes and do all the requirements, I have the mindset to do all my work with motivation. The third idea is paying attention. This idea was challenging because I could not fouce for a while if I were studying or learning, so I tried to combine my other theory with this so each idea would support the other. For example, if I lose my attention, I try to find patterns in my learning so I can stay motivated or give myself five minutes of a break to not get bored. Over, all those strategies help.
