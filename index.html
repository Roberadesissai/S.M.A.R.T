BOOK

The Book that helps me my learning and gets motivated in all my classes and to get a better understanding of all courses is"The New Science Of Learning" by Terry Doyle and Todd Zakrajsek. This Book talks about how there are many ways to understand what you learn in class or anywhere, And it shows you the best strategy to use in any circumstance. One of the strategies I found most interesting that I start using is "Find a pattern in learning," so basically, the strategy is about how you connect what you learn with something and find what makes them the same or different if you can identify that you understand. This is one of many things that I have learned from this Book; there is a lot you should read this Book if you want to understand your learning differently and efficiently. There is another book that helped me as well, and this Book is called "College Student Skills". This Book tells you how to manage your time, stress, take notes, and so on. I recommend those books if you want to move forward in your success.
